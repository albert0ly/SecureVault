Look at the top-right area of Solution Explorer. You'll see several small icons:

- **A folder with dots/files inside**, or
- **A document/page icon**

## How to Find It (If Unsure)

1. **Hover over each toolbar button** in Solution Explorer
2. **Look for tooltip that says:** "Show All Files"
3. **Click it**
